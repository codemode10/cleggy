How to get there?

We recommend hiring a car (as a couple or as 2 couples) as there is no public transport in the Queyras. The road is about 3hrs from the wedding venue to reach the chalet, and it is very beautiful. If there are issues on the car front, please let us know and we can try and help organizing the rides.
